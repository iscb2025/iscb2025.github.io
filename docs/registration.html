Registration

We have reached full capacity for on-site attendance. Only virtual attendance is now possible!

Join the waiting list for on-site attendance should slots become available.

REGISTER HERE


For questions regarding registration, please first consult the frequently-asked-question (FAQ) page, and only then contact registrations@iscb2025.info.

The fees for the 2025 conference are as follows (see Note 1 for details on the categories, and the terms and conditions for registration):

Conference

Virtual attendance entitlements

  • Access to live streams of the scientific sessions
  • Access to Q&A during live streams
  • Access to the Conference proceedings

Hybrid attendance entitlements

  • Attendance to all scientific sessions
  • Access to live streams
  • Conference material
  • Access to the Exhibition Area
  • Coffee breaks & lunches
  • Welcome Reception attendance
  • Access to the Conference proceedings

Non-ISCB Member 650 EUR
Reduced fees ISCB Member 325 EUR
Non-ISCB Member 410 EUR

Note 1: Fee category conditions
  • Member fees are applicable only if membership status is active. Membership status will be verified and followed up by the ISCB Office. Please address the ISCB Office at office@iscb.international for membership queries.
  • Reduced fees apply to low-income and medium-low-income countries considered, according to the World Bank list for the fiscal year 2025).
  • For student registrations, a valid student ID / letter from supervisor needs to be provided through the online registration process. We reserve the right to allocate your registration to the appropriate category, in case relevant documentation is missing or is not valid.
  • Retired or Senior (65+ y.o.) registration category applies ONLY for those registered to ISCB as “Retired/65+” for 2025. Membership status will be verified and followed up by the ISCB Office. Please address the ISCB Office at office@iscb.international for membership queries.
